The Dining Dispatch: Top Matcha Spots in Paris

🍴 Kosyuen

Just steps from the Louvre, Kosyuen, a Japanese tea salon, is where matcha dreams come true. From Uji and Shizuoka, gyokuro, sencha, hojicha, genmaicha – it’s a green tea masterclass. But the real star? Their praline matcha, so rich and decadent it’s basically dessert in disguise.

📍 Address: 258, rue Saint-Honoré, 75001 Paris

🚇 Metro: Tuileries / Palais-Royal

💵 Price: From 7.50€ / Matcha

🍴 MiniCafé

Tucked away on Île Saint-Louis, Minicafé proves that great things come in small packages. This tiny coffee shop delivers big on matcha: from tonic for the bold to dirty for the indulgent. A green, cool escape in a setting as charming as it is intimate.

📍 Address: 1, rue des Deux Ponts, 75004 Paris

🚇 Metro: Pont-Marie

💵 Price: From 6€ / Matcha

🍴 Lactem

In the 9th arrondissement, Lactem Café is where matcha gets a fancy twist. From creamy lattes to detox smoothies (matcha, almond, coconut, banana, vanilla – you name it), everything here is as delicious as it is Instagram-worthy. Pair it with a matcha white chocolate cookie, and you’ve found your new favorite indulgence spot.

Several Locations

🚇 Metro: Notre-Dame-de-Lorreyte / Palais Royal

💵 Price: From 6.5€ / Matcha

🍴 iRASSHAi

Just steps from the Louvre, iRASSHAi is Paris’s ultimate hub for Japanese cuisine. A mix of concept store, gourmet grocery, café, and bar, it offers a full immersion into Japanese culture. Sip on Uji matcha by day and matcha cocktails by night when the Kissaten transforms into the Sakaba. With teas, snacks, and workshops, you’ll never leave empty-handed.

📍 Address: 40, rue du Louvre, 75001 Paris

🚇 Metro: Les Halles

💵 Price: From 6.5€ / Matcha

🍴 Lupicia

In the heart of the 6th arrondissement, Lupicia is the pinnacle of Japanese tea mastery. Offering a prestigious selection of matchas like Kurama and Satsuma, this tea house is a haven for connoisseurs. Settle into their elegant tea salon to savor these treasures alongside exquisite pastries.

📍 Address: 40, rue Bonaparte, 75006 Paris

🚇 Metro: Saint-Germain-Des-Prés

💵 Price: From 6€ / Matcha

🍴 Mori Café

In the 11th, Mori Café reinvents Japanese cuisine with a 100% vegan twist. Here, matcha reigns supreme: creamy oat milk lattes, matchagato (ice cream + matcha shot, pure genius), and ceremonial matcha cookies. A green, ethical, and indulgent pause that takes you straight to Japan.

📍 Address: 2, rue des Taillandiers, 75011 Paris

🚇 Metro: Bastille

💵 Price: From 6€ / Matcha

🍴 Kodama

In the heart of the 2nd, Kodama turns matcha into an experience. Their organic powder from Kyushu transforms into silky lattes or vibrant drinks, perfect for a pick-me-up. In this elegant tea bar, every cup tells a story – and it’s always a beautiful shade of green.

📍 Address: 30, rue Tiquetonne, 75002 Paris

🚇 Metro: Etienne-Marcel

💵 Price: From 6.5€ / Matcha

🍴 Café Caractère

In the 11th, Café Caractère lives up to its name: no compromises on flavor here. The matcha latte, smooth and perfectly balanced, is an invitation to unwind in a sleek, minimalist setting. Proof that elegance and caffeine are a match made in heaven.

📍 Address: 2, rue des Taillandiers, 75011 Paris

🚇 Metro: Bastille

💵 Price: From 6€ / Matcha

🍴 Buddy Buddy

In the 10th, Buddy Buddy is the go-to spot for bold flavor lovers. Their Matcha Buddy – a blend of ceremonial matcha, peanut butter, and oat milk – is as indulgent as it is addictive. Perfect for stocking up on winter reserves, no calorie fears allowed.

📍 Address: 15, rue de Marseille, 75010 Paris

🚇 Metro: Jacques Bonsergent

💵 Price: 6.80€ / Matcha

🍴 Laïzé

In the 3rd, Laïzé is the ultimate haven for matcha and bubble tea lovers. The name? “Pop in anytime,” and trust me, once you’re in, you won’t want to leave. From ultra-smooth matcha lattes to bubble teas that are mini Taiwanese masterpieces, they know how to treat you right. A sweet, stylish escape blending tradition and cool vibes.

Several Locations

💵 Price: From 7.10€ / Matcha
